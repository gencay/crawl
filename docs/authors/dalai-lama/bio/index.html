Dalai Lama \u2014 Full Biography

1935 — Present

The 14th Dalai Lama, Tenzin Gyatso, is the current Dalai Lama. He is the spiritual leader of Tibetan Buddhism and was awarded the Nobel Peace Prize in 1989 for his non-violent struggle for the liberation of Tibet.
