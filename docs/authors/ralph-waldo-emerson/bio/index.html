Ralph Waldo Emerson \u2014 Full Biography

1803 — 1882

Ralph Waldo Emerson was an American essayist, lecturer, philosopher, and poet who led the transcendentalist movement of the mid-19th century. He was a champion of individualism and a prescient critic of societal pressures.
